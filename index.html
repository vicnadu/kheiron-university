Scholarships

Strategy Scholarship

In Kheíron University, strategy is one of the biggest virtues among students and professors. To receive this scholarship, the pupil must prove to have strategy skills in any form he/she/they want. It may be through a map, a simulation, a path in the forest...the choice has to be personal, but also smart.

Academics Scholarship

Academics is the base for all types of knowledge. Students that have exceptional scholastic performance are strongly encouraged to apply for an Academics Scholarship.

Puzzle Scholarship

At Kheíron University, not a single soul runs away from challenges. Prove your ability and logic skills solving an intricate puzzle never solved before. Pupils that get to the end of the puzzle, are contemplated with a full ride scholarship.

Traditions

Shooting burning arrows in the sky on the prom: Yes, you heard it right, in the prom, one of the most special moments in your life, you will have the opportunity to shoot a burning arrow in the sky to celebrate your journey at Kheíron University.
Some say that the process to get into a university is like climbing a mountain, you pass through a lot of problems in your climb, but then, you get at the top, you can appreciate the view and you know that it was all worth it. Now, if you want to join a fraternity, well, let’s just say that the mountain won’t be a metaphorical one. Do you really want to join a fraternity? Prove it. And don’t worry, if you hear thunder above you, it’s probably just Zeus cheering for you.

Vision

Kheíron University sets the standard for liberal arts and sciences education. We have committed to creating and sustaining the conditions that enable all Kherion University students to experience a transformative journey, that shape them personally and academically.

Mission

Our mission is to leave a legacy for those who joined our university. Help the students to become true leaders and impact society through their knowledge acquired in the Kheíron Community.

Values

We believe in the power to belong to a community. As the African proverbs say: “if you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go further, go with others”. What moves us is our legacy, because together we are stronger, in the midst of the unexpected. We get strength and go beyond reason Exuding originality and thirst for knowledge, masters in showing the true connection.
